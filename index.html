🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Fluentex stores your chosen username, country flag, XP score, streak count, quiz history, badge progress, and performance statistics. This data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage / sessionStorage).

2. Global Leaderboard

If you participate in the global leaderboard, your username, flag emoji, XP score, and streak count are stored in a shared database visible to all users. We recommend using a nickname rather than your real name.

3. AI API Usage

Quiz questions, flashcards, and Word of the Day are generated via the Anthropic Claude API. Your selected level and category are sent to generate relevant content. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage and sessionStorage to persist your learning progress across sessions. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to operate the leaderboard feature.

6. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you clear your browser storage. Leaderboard entries may be retained for up to 12 months.

7. Children's Privacy

Fluentex is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents may clear their child's data by clearing browser storage.
